## 2. Privacy Policy
Your privacy is extremely important to us.
- **Local Security & 100% Privacy**: FUNRC is a pure client-side application. Your TikTok Client Key, Client Secret, and Access Tokens are stored securely and locally on your own computer. We **DO NOT** collect, store, or transmit your personal data or credentials to any external servers.
- **Data Protection**: All video uploads and account tokens are only transmitted directly to the official TikTok API endpoints via secure HTTPS protocols.
